There have been nearly 2.9 million views of the data portal. With the rise in the number of datasets, there is a corresponding increase in the popularity. Throughout 2013, the data portal averaged 109,533 visitors per month, compared to an average 80,039 through 2012. Yet, the largest number of visitors in a single month was in June 2011 when the City released a comprehensive dataset of crimes.
